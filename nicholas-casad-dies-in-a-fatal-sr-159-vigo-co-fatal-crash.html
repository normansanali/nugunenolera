Nicholas Casad Dies in a Fatal SR 159 Vigo Co. fatal crash

July 04, 2024
blog

The person who perished in a late-morning collision in Vigo County on Sunday has been identified.

Nicholas Casad, a 20-year-old Terre Haute resident, was the fatal driver, according to Sheriff John Plasse.

Deputies were summoned to a single-vehicle crash at State Road 159, just north of Harlan Drive, at 11 a.m., according to a Vigo County sheriff’s social media post.

On 159, Casad was traveling north when he crossed over into the southbound lane, skidded through the grass, and collided with a tree.
