HANNAH Montana actor Mitchel Musso has been arrested in Rockwall, Texas, and appears unrecognizable in his mugshot.

The U.S. Sun can confirm the arrest happened on Saturday night when Mitchel, 32, was taken to the Texas jail for "public drunkenness and theft."

According to the Rockwall jail records obtained by The U.S. Sun, Mitchel was booked on Saturday, August 26, with five charges.

According to TMZ, Mitchel had been acting "belligerent" and "stole an item from a food market."

Cops reportedly arrived outside of a hotel where Mitchel was under the influence of alcohol and stole a bag of chips.

It turned out that Mitchel also had a slew of outstanding traffic warrants in his name.

ROUGH NIGHT

In his mugshot, the Disney actor appeared tired and confused.

He scrunched his forehead, lifted his eyebrows, and appeared to need to squint his eyes to see in the bright lights of the police station.

Reportedly, Mitchel is charged with public intoxication, theft (under $100), expired resignation, failure to display a driver's license, an violating a promise to appear notice from the incident.

TMZ claimed Mitchel is being released on a $1,000 bond.

Mitchel is best known for playing Oliver Oken on Disney's Hannah Montana, co-starting beside Miley Cyrus.

After Hannah Montana, Mitchel played the lead role of King Brady in Pair of Kings from 2010 to 2012, and he also hosted Prankstars during that time.

One of his main gigs has been as a voice actor on Phineas and Ferb from 2007 to 2015 and he reprised his role again in 2020 for Phineas and Ferb the Movie: Candace Against the Universe.

Aside from his acting work, Mitchel has also released his own music.

He released his self-titled debut album in 2009 and followed it up with a live album, Live at the Fillmore in 2010.
