How much are the tickets for the Kentucky Derby in 2024?

September 15, 2024
blog

The Kentucky Derby is one of the most prestigious events in the world of horse racing, drawing in thousands of spectators from around the globe. If you're considering attending the 150th Run for the Roses at Churchill Downs in 2024, it's essential to understand the various ticket options available and their associated pricing. In this guide, we'll delve into the comprehensive details of the ticket prices for the 2024 Kentucky Derby, including different seating options and their amenities.

General Admission and Infield Tickets

Infield General Admission

  • Price: Starting at $275
  • Details: This ticket provides access to the infield area, where spectators can bring their own chairs or picnic blankets and watch the races from the world's largest 4K video board. It includes access to upgraded restrooms, dedicated concessions, mutuel lines, and a racing program. Food and beverage concessions are available for purchase on the day of the event.

Infield Final Turn General Admission

  • Price: $275 for two-day Kentucky Oaks and Kentucky Derby tickets
  • Details: This ticket includes a race day program and access to the infield and final turn area, where spectators can enjoy a standing room view of the races from a private area. It also provides access to the world's largest 4K video board, upgraded restrooms, concessions, and mutuel lines.

Frontside Plaza Walkaround

  • Price: Starting at $693 for both the Kentucky Oaks and Kentucky Derby
  • Details: This ticket offers a reimagined view of the Paddock with standing room access and frontside amenities. It includes an all-inclusive food and beverage menu, off-site parking access, access to first-floor restrooms and wagering machines, and a walk-around access to the plaza and Paddock viewing platforms. Spectators can watch the races on new video boards, although direct sight lines to the track itself are not included.

New Paddock Seating

The reimagined Paddock at Churchill Downs introduces new seating options, each offering unique experiences.

  • Price: Starting at $693 for both the Kentucky Oaks and Kentucky Derby
  • Details: The most affordable ticket for the new Paddock experience is a general admission ticket that includes a first-floor standing-room view of the Paddock with access to first-floor amenities, such as bathrooms, concessions, and wagering machines. This ticket also provides access to the plaza and includes food and drink.

Reserved Seating

  • Price: Starting at $650
  • Details: Reserved seating options offer premium views of the racetrack and outdoor racing, providing a traditional and comfortable way to experience the Kentucky Derby.

Dining

  • Price: Starting at $1,375
  • Details: The dining experience at the Kentucky Derby offers gourmet meals, fine beverages, and an unforgettable culinary experience for attendees.

Private Suites

  • Price: Starting at $135,000
  • Details: Private suite accommodations at the Kentucky Derby offer some of the finest amenities in professional sports, providing a luxurious and exclusive way to enjoy the event.

Premium Seating Options

For those seeking an elevated and luxurious experience at the Kentucky Derby, premium seating options are available.

  • Price: Ranging from $1,495 to $5,995 per person
  • Details: Premium covered and uncovered seating options offer excellent views of the racetrack and come with all-inclusive food and beverage menus. Prices vary based on the specific section, row, and coverage.

Additional Ticketing Information

  • The Kentucky Derby tickets can be purchased through the official website, kentuckyderby.com, which outlines various seating options and associated amenities and experiences.
  • Ticketmaster hosts the official fan-to-fan resale marketplace for Kentucky Derby and Kentucky Oaks tickets.
  • Derby Experiences offers various packages that combine Derby and Oaks tickets with access to exclusive lounges, hotel accommodations, transportation, and more, with package prices ranging from $2,999 to $12,091 per person.

The 2024 Kentucky Derby offers a wide range of ticket options to suit different preferences and budgets. From general admission and infield tickets to premium seating, dining experiences, and private suites, attendees have the opportunity to tailor their Derby experience according to their preferences and budget. The pricing of tickets varies based on the specific seating option, amenities provided, and the duration of attendance, offering something for everyone who wishes to be part of this iconic event.
