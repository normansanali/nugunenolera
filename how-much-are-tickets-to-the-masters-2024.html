How much are tickets to the masters 2024?

July 20, 2024
blog

The Masters Tournament is one of the most prestigious events in the world of golf, attracting fans and enthusiasts from across the globe.

As the 90th edition of the tournament approaches, many are eager to secure tickets to witness this grand event unfold at the historic Augusta National Golf Club in Augusta, GA.

But with original standard tickets sold out long ago, the availability and pricing of resale tickets have become the focal point of attention for those hoping to attend the competition in person.

Understanding Masters 2024 Ticket Prices

Original Standard Tickets

Original standard tickets for the Masters 2024 were sold through a lottery system the year before the event.

These tickets, which are no longer available, were reported to have been priced at approximately $100 to $140, depending on whether they were for practice or competition days.

Resale Tickets

Given the high demand and scarcity of original standard tickets, resale tickets have become the primary option for individuals seeking entry into the Masters 2024. Resale tickets for the tournament are available on platforms such as StubHub, Vivid Seats, and Ticketmaster.

Pricing Analysis

The resale ticket prices for the Masters 2024 vary based on the specific dates and the type of packages available. Here's a breakdown of the resale ticket prices based on the information available:

  • Single-Day Tickets:

    • The lowest resale ticket prices for the Masters 2024 start at $1,325 for the first practice day of the tournament.
    • For individual tournament rounds, the prices range from approximately $1,649 to $2,126, depending on the specific date.

  • Multi-Day Packages:

    • Resale websites offer 2-day and 4-day packages for both practice and competition days.
    • The 2-day packages start at $3,532, while the 4-day packages start at $5,119.

  • Ticket Fluctuations:

    • The past month has seen fluctuation in resale ticket listing prices, with some prices increasing while others have decreased. It's important to note that the availability and pricing of resale tickets may continue to change leading up to the tournament.

Ticket Categories on StubHub

StubHub, one of the prominent platforms for purchasing resale tickets, offers various ticket categories for the Masters 2024. These categories include tickets for practice rounds, individual tournament rounds, 2-Day Passes, and 4-Day Passes. The availability of these categories provides potential attendees with flexibility in choosing the type of access they desire.

Insight into Ticket Availability and Purchase Procedures

Original Standard Tickets

The original standard tickets for the Masters 2024 were sold through a lottery system, and the availability for these tickets is no longer viable for this year's tournament. 

But, individuals interested in attending future tournaments can create an account for next year's lottery on the official Masters website when applications open in June.

Resale Tickets

As the primary remaining option for attending the tournament in person, resale tickets are available for purchase on various platforms.

Among these, StubHub stands out as a prominent marketplace for securing resale tickets to the Masters 2024.

Individuals can explore the available ticket categories and make their purchases based on their preferences and budget.

Potential Discounts and Price Changes

The pricing of resale tickets for the Masters 2024 has seen fluctuations in the past month, with some ticket prices decreasing.

This presents a potential opportunity for individuals to monitor the market and make informed decisions based on the evolving ticket prices.

It's advisable for interested individuals to stay updated on the available ticket options and be mindful of any potential discounts or price changes leading up to the tournament.

The Masters 2024 presents an exciting opportunity for golf enthusiasts and spectators to witness world-class competition at the historic Augusta National Golf Club. While original standard tickets are no longer available, the resale ticket market offers access to the tournament, albeit at a premium.

Understanding the pricing tiers, ticket categories, availability, and purchase procedures is essential for those seeking to secure tickets to the event.

The allure of the Masters tournament, coupled with the exclusivity of attending in person, makes the exploration of ticket pricing a crucial aspect for individuals eager to be part of this celebrated event.
