Bill O’Brien Bio, Parents, Wife, Children, Siblings: Bill O’Brien, officially known as William James O’Brien is an American football coach born on October 23, 1969, in Dorchester, Boston, Massachusetts, United States.

He developed a love for American football at an early age and as a child, he played Little League. Bill schooled at St. John’s Preparatory School in Danvers and after completing, he attended Brown University where he played defensive end and linebacker for the Brown Bears from 1990 to 1992.
