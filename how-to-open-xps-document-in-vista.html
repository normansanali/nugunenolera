How do I open an XPS document?

You may access the Viewer directly, or open it through the file.
  • Open the XPS Viewer first. Click the Start button, then “All Programs” and “XPS Viewer” in the menu.
  • Go to “File,” then “Open.”
  • Browse to find your file. Click “Open” to load it.

    • What programs can open XPS files?

    NOTE: XPS files can be opened with Microsoft’s XPS Viewer, which is included with Windows Vista, Windows 7, Windows 8, and Internet Explorer 6 or later. It is also part of the XPS Essentials Pack, which can be downloaded from Microsoft and used with Windows XP.

    Why can’t I open an XPS document?

    As a matter of fact, if you can’t open . xps files, this could be because multiple programs (most likely, your browser) are attempting to open the same file at the same time. To avoid such issues, set XPS Viewer as your default program to view and read . xps files.
