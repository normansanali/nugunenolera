Who won Head of Household this week on Big Brother 25?

September 24, 2024
blog

Warning: this article contains spoilers for Big Brother 25‘s live eviction episode, which aired on Nov. 2.

With only four houseguests remaining in the 100-day battle for Big Brother 25’s $750,000 grand prize, seizing power is more important than ever. And when the dust settled at the end of the eviction episode on Thursday night, one player emerged as the newest Head of Household.

After Cirie Fields was unanimously voted out, Felicia Cannon, Bowie Jane Ball, and Jag Bains competed in week 14’s first live competition while the outgoing Head of Household, Matt Klotz, waited on the sidelines.

Host Julie Chen Moonves had the three players observe several pages from the “Chenbot” comic book. Answering “A” or “B,” the houseguests were asked seven questions about what they saw on the pages. By the end of the seventh question, Jag and Bowie Jane tied for first and moved into a sudden-death round. The allies were asked to calculate the number of seconds a certain competition from earlier in the season lasted. And without going over, the houseguest closest to the right answer won.

Well, as history has it, Bowie Jane won her third tie-breaker of the season and was handed the Head of Household key. The 46-year-old DJ and barrister has moved into the private suite three times since the beginning of week 10, and her most recent triumph guaranteed her a one-in-three shot at winning Big Brother 25. She also tied Cameron Hardin’s record for the highest number of Head of Household reigns this season.

There are only three episodes remaining this season, and the final four will shrink to the final three during a special live eviction on Sunday night.
