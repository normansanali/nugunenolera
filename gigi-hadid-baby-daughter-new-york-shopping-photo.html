Gigi Hadid Wears Louis Vuitton in New York with Her Baby Girl

August 20, 2024
blog

Having welcomed her first child with Zayn Malik earlier this year, Gigi Hadid has been spotted with her new daughter on only a handful occasions, as the model continues to keep a low profile since becoming a first-time mom.

Earlier this week, the model posed in the snow with her baby, and was also photographed walking in New York with her daughter for the very first time. And on Saturday, Hadid hit the city's shops with her baby daughter in a stroller.

As per usual, Hadid looked seriously chic in a long Thom Browne ribbed coat, a white dress shirt, faded jeans, and a pair of Doc Martens. She completed the casual outfit with a shearling bucket hat by Louis Vuitton, and a black face mask.

Moryc Welt / BACKGRID

Back in October, a source opened up to Us Weekly about how Hadid and Malik are adjusting to being new parents. The source revealed, "Gigi and Zayn are loving parenthood and can’t get enough of their little girl ... Things are going really swimmingly between them and they’re both so happy. Gigi is so excited to be a new mom and has been appreciating every moment."

Announcing their daughter's birth in September, Malik made it clear that he's going to be every inch the doting dad. "Our baby girl is here, healthy & beautiful," he wrote. "To try put into words how I am feeling right now would be an impossible task. The love I feel for this tiny human is beyond my understanding. Grateful to know her, proud to call her mine, & thankful for the life we will have together x."
