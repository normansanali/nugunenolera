WWE Announces Supplemental Draft Picks On SmackDown LowDown

July 03, 2024
blog

The landscape of the continues to change.

The 2023 WWE Draft kicked off last night on WWE SmackDown, shifting multiple wrestlers between the red and blue brands.

Today on the SmackDown LowDown on Peacock, several more moves were announced in the supplemental draft. Here’s a rundown of what went down:

  • The Viking Raiders
  • Candice LeRae
  • Dexter Lumis
  • Maximum Male Models
  • Apollo Crews
  • Natalya
  • Chelsea Green
  • Sonya Deville
  • Zoey Stark
  • JD McDonagh

Drafted to SmackDown:

  • Hit Row
  • Lacey Evans

Officially free agents (meaning they can appear anywhere):

  • Omos w/ MVP
  • Mustafa Ali
  • Dolph Ziggler
  • Von Wagner

The 2023 WWE Draft will continue this Monday night on RAW.
