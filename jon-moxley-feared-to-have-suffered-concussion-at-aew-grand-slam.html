Jon Moxley Feared To Have Suffered Concussion At AEW Grand Slam

August 14, 2024
blog

Jon Moxley lost the AEW International Title to Rey Fenix at Dynamite Grand Slam. The end of the match seemed very awkward, and nobody knew what was going on. Now, we know a bit more about the situation.

Click here for our complete coverage of AEW Dynamite Grand Slam.

The ending of the International Title match was very interesting. It seemed as if they botched the finish, as Jon Moxley did not kick out of a finisher, but the referee stopped counting anyway. Then Fenix did the same move again and he got the win.

PW Insider reports that Jon Moxley was able to make it back without anyone’s help. He was walking on his own, but he was checked on right away. It is now believed that he may have suffered a concussion.

As you may have seen, the finish to Jon Moxley vs. Rey Fenix went awry when Moxley did not kick out after a move as planned.

Fenix quickly nailed Moxley again and scored the three count, winning the AEW International title.

Moxley was checked by doctors for a long time before sitting up and walking out.

Backstage, the belief is Moxley may have suffered a concussion.  More as we confirm.

Jon Moxley just won the AEW International Title from Orange Cassidy at All Out. This was a shocking loss for Mox in Arthur Ashe Stadium. We also have to wonder if this was the actual intended finish for the match.
