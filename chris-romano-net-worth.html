What is Chris Romano's Net Worth?

Chris Romano is an American actor, writer, producer, and director who has a net worth of $6 million. Chris Romano was born in Nashua, New Hampshire in February 1978. After attending Nashua High School South he attended Plymout State College and eventually graduated from Emerson College in 2000. He is best known for co-creating the television series Blue Mountain State on Spike. Blue Mountain State ran 39 episodes over three seasons between 2010 and 2011 and has developed a cult following after being released on Netflix.

Chris  also served as an executive producer, writter, and actor (starring as Sammy Cacciatore) on the series. Romano has also worked as a producer or director for the TV series Acceptable TV, The Sarah Silverman Program., How I Met Your Mother, and more. He also had a recurring role on How I Met Your Mother as Punchy from 2008 to 2011. He was a co-executive producer of 24 episodes of How I Met Your Mother.
